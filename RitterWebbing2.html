German “coconut” canteen (Kokosflasche)

Not for sale

Officially just another type of the M31 Feldflasche (standard WWII German canteen), but with a unique pressed fiber shell. Issued mainly during the early to mid-war years but used over all war years and all war regions(seen 1941–42, 43 and 45 markings, but limited numbers overall).

This canteen issued in some early post-war years in about 1952-1954 range

Has no difference from war-time issued except stamps

Ritter Alfurit D.R.P.a.

Heinrich Ritter, Aluminiumwarenfabrik, Esslingen/Neckar
